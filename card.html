HOW TO PLAY?

This ia a card guessing game which have total 10 cards of marvel superheroes, two of each type i.e five sets. You need to click a card and then guess where you can find the other pair of your card.If you guess right your score will increase by 1 in every right guess but if you fail you loose all of your points.So, you need to be good at guessing game .
HAVE FUN
